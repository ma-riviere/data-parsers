Here we concentrate on strengthening veterans into the honed weapons we need to break Tiamaranta's defenses.

If you're interested in perfecting your combat prowess and teamwork skills, I encourage you to step right up.

 "So what is this 'Crucible'?" "I've heard enough."

The Empyrean Crucible's a simulated battlefield where you and your group fight opponents in multiple increasingly difficult rounds.

If you're defeated, you enter the Ready Room, where you may either wait for the end of the battle, or rejoin, using a Worthiness Ticket.

If at least one of the group emerges victorious, the Recordkeeper judges whether you move on or not. It's that simple.
