What is it?

We may save the odd Kinah by lowering ceilings, and swapping out gold for silver, but where does it stop?

Would it better if we designed grey, cubic buildings? What would architects come to call my era? The Poor Period? The Bland Period? The Flat Period?

No. I'll have none of it. We must advance our arts if we are to advance our way of life. Accounts be damned.

 "That's the spirit!"
