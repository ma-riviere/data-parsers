Galam. Have you come to see my display items?

I see people's looks of awe over what I craft, but I don't see my items with those same eyes. There is no perfection in the world of crafting.

Even master crafters have mishaps, make bad choices, suffer bad luck. Some would say it's those imperfections that make a piece special. Perfection is simply that fantasy for which we strive, off somewhere in a faraway place.

Even knowing we can never reach it, we push nonetheless. We're like little clowns on a long journey searching for the rainbow.

 "Wait... clowns? On a journey? What?"
