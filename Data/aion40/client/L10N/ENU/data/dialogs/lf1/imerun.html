Well, after war broke out in the Abyss. Everyone was terrified. We'd been ushered into a strange new age of tension and fear. But, we never thought it would appear right on our doorsteps...

Then strange creatures attacked. These were no scouting parties. People were dying. We received no help, no support from anywhere. In the end, a cadre of volunteers stepped up to defend the village. What they lacked in experience, they made up in spirit, determination, and loyalty.

Those courageous people, bound by a pact to defend the innocent, were the founders of the Tolbas Watch.
