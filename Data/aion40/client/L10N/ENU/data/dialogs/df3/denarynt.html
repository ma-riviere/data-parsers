Yes, yes, Azphel's shadow...

I never expected to live like this.

I used to wear fine clothing and perfectly coiffured hair.

Now? Rags and braids.

 "It sounds like it was a fine life." "I'm sorry for your loss."

Shugo merchants brought me the latest fashions from Pandaemonium--brought them straight to my home.

I had to leave all that behind. I fled blindly when the undead overran the land.

Somewhere, in my home, there's a wardrobe filled with fine clothing. And none of it will help me light this blasted fire.

 "Aion grant you strength."
