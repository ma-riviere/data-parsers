Arieluma!

Welcome to the Lyceum, the great temple of Sanctum.

The Lyceum is a sacred place where we come together to serve our fellow Daevas and offer up our praises to the Five Seraphim Lords who watch over us eternally.

 "Tell me of the Seraphim Lords." "Farewell."

Five are they, the Lords of Light, who stand sentinel over the shattered orb of Atreia!

They represent the five essential values: Light, Justice, Liberty, Illusion, and Life.

They are not just distant figures of worship. They sometimes manifest here in Sanctum in person. I have no words to describe such beauty. You are never the same again after you've beheld one of the Seraphim Lords in their full glory.

 "Prosper in the light."
