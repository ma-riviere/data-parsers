As the Reians say, "Your eyes to my eyes."

Assume a waterfall.

If we both stand in front of it, are we looking at the same thing? Do we each perceive the subject differently?

Perhaps you concentrate on the frothing descent, whereas I see the placid stream at the bottom.

And if we do see different things, is that a trick of the Empyrean Lords? Or is it because of our previous experiences which turn us into different people?

 "You're more a philosopher than a dreamer."
