Hello Daeva. Welcome to Misty Vista Villa, er Village.

The village is named for the mist that seems to shroud both it and the road almost all the time.

It is an odd thing, to be standing on the road in the middle of a thick mist. It can make you ask questions you do not ask yourself in bright sunlight. Like "Is this the life I really wanted?" Shugos are supposed to make lots of money, but we gardeners are paid so little for such hard work.
