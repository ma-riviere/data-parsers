Welcome to Millennium Tree Village, Daeva.

Heard the legend about the Millennium Tree that the village is named for, yes?

Daeva has not? Well, many years ago, a Brigade General of some legion lost a battle during the Millenium War and came to this village to rest.

He set down cane near entrance of the village while he rested, but while he slept, his cane sprouted and began to grow into a great tree! Tree is now the centerpiece of village! Our pride and joy.
