Then join me, friend. Crab Fest arrives!

Have you heard the tale of how Aion came to Asmodae disguised as a Potcrab?

Once on a time, there lived a widow with her seven sons. The eldest six went off to make their fame and fortune as Daevas, while the seventh stayed home with his mother.

One day, the seventh son came home carrying a crab. "Mother!" he cried. "This Potcrab says..."

 "But Potcrabs don't talk."
