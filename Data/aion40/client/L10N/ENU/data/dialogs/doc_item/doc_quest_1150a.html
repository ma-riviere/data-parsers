Dear Qydro,

Have you heard from your Uncle Belbua recently?

He wrote me ten days ago, promising to visit. However, I've neither seen nor heard from him since.

I'm so worried about him I can scarcely sleep, but I'm old--I can't just go wandering around looking for him.

There's something more. I've heard that a suspicious bunch of men has been seen skulking around his farm.

I'd like to ask you to check on Belbua.

Your old father won't sleep well until he finds out what has happened to his brother.
