Redemption Demand Note

To: Une

From: Numonerk

To Une, Guardian Legionary

We are sorry to inform you that your payment is overdue.

In addition, we deem your failing to inform us of your job transfer to be a breach of good faith, and must emphasize we have no intention of granting an extension.

We urge you to pay the 100,000 Kinah for the goods, or to make an equivalent reimbursement.

Three (3) treasure boxes from the shipwreck on the Cantas Coast will be considered equivalent to the above amount.

Please understand that we will be in a position to inform the Brigade General of your failure to make a payment unless these obligations are fulfilled as soon as possible.

- Scribed by Ivis, Weapon Merchant, on behalf of Numonerk
