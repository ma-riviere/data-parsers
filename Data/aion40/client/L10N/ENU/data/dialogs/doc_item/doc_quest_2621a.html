Dear brother Freyr,

Sorry for my late reply, brother.

It is good to hear that everyone is fine over there.

I have been well too.

Except for the weather. As you guessed, it is really cold here.

The quilted clothes you sent me last time was of a great help to me. It keeps me warm. Whenever I wear it, I think of you and other family members.

As you know, things get only worse here in Beluslan.

The situation is beyond your imagination. Yes, I know that you hear the news. But you can realize the seriousness of the situation only when you come here and feel it.

I try not to be relaxed and to be careful all the time.

Good news is that I can compete with outstanding people here and I am actually proving my abilities...

But as things are going this way, I cannot help but turn you down.

The Malek mine is quite far from where I am. Furthermore, I am a centurion. I cannot leave my post for such a personal matter.

I think it would be better for you to ask a Shugo directly.

I have more things to tell you, but someone is calling me now.

I will write you again next time.

Good bye, and drop me a line once in a while.

From Beluslan, Freyja...

PS:

Wait! Next time you write a letter, send it by a person.

That Shugo express mail service delivered your last letter to a wrong person. It took me quite long to get it back.
