Day 1

I received orders from the headquarters, stating that we will mass produce RM-12b here.

Preparations start today.

We have already inventoried necessary materials and tools, and requested supplies.

Day 2

This new equipment is not easy to install. The entire day was wasted by equipment failures.

We followed the instructions but it didn't work. There must be something missing from the manual.

If the situation doesn't improve, we'll ask the RM to send someone. I don't know how long assistance from Heiron will take to arrive.

Day 6

Production of the test subject Pretors began three days ago; so far all Pretors are defective.

We must produce at least one valid subject Pretor in order to fix the problem.

While still short-handed, we expect a viable subject in three days. I have heard that no one wants to come here.

Day 10

We still fail to produce viable Pretors. If this keeps up, we will never make it to the next stage.

Continued delays will, no doubt, result in a reprimand, or worse.

The RM thinks that the researchers sent during the earlier stages should be sufficient. If a breakthrough test subject is discovered, they will demand that we commence production immediately.
