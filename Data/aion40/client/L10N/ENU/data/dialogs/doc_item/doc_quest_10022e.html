Operation Report 9: Inggison Outpost Operation Status Report, Day 00 of Inggison invasion.

- Guardians in Inggison Outpost carry orders to sweep Mephistis Farm in two teams.

- Under Operation T, one team is raiding the farm. Under Operation U, the other team is destroying all the Drana.

- Wisplight Legionaries confiscated Balaur document re: Drana. Document includes plan to transport Drana from Mephistis Farm to Tiamaranta.

- Alchemy Master Diana formulated acid from Glands and Fly Agaric Juice. Poison successfully deployed.

- Finding: Collaboration with civilian Daevas key to operation.

3. Plans and Conclusions: Order issued to manufacture mass quantities of Alchemy Master Diana's acid before advancing to Tiamaranta.

- Acid will be sprayed throughout Drana farms to weaken enemy power base.

- Though no force nor poison initially on hand, Inggison Outpost successfully completed mission objectives and found new battle method going forward. - End of Report, Centurion Iaetia
