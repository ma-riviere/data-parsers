- Peace Treaty Proposal -

To the Altgard Fortress Chieftain

Akagitan, Chieftain of the Red Mane Tribe, will make peace with the Asmodians.

Unlike the other Mau Tribes, we Red Manes do not bend to the will of the Balaur.

We have no reason to stand against the Asmodians. This war is meaningless and should not be fought.

The Red Mane Tribe is no longer brothers with the other Mau tribes, no longer slaves to the Balaur. We will become the allies of the Asmodians.

We will stand together against the Balaur. To show our truth-telling, we send ambassadors to talk terms.

- Red Mane Chieftain Akagitan -
