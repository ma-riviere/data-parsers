Month 3 Day 7

Location: Reshanta Northwest Floating Island, Number 35

--Mumukin snuck into the warehouse again. Found him in the morning. He's cleaning the toilet as punishment.

--The prison guard leader tried to get friendly Madam Bovariki. She beat him until we intervened. He was taken to the medical unit.

Month 3 Day 15

Location: Reshanta North Floating Island, Number 43-6

--Anchored in the Floating Island and captured a few creatures in hopes they turn out to be profitable.

--Engineer Lahulahu and engineers rose in mutiny, demanding a raise in salary. They refused to move the ship until they got it. The captain was furious.

--Accidental explosion in the gun deck damaged three firearms. Chief Gunner Koakoa wrote letters to explain the situation.

Month 3 Day 23

Location: Reshanta Northeast Floating Island, Number 12

--Plundered a Black Cloud Traders cargo ship. Obtained jewelry including Malek, Platinum, and Mithril. Also found some food.

--Ship crashed into the Floating Island. Stern was damaged, but we managed to repair it.

Month 3 Day 29

Location: Reshanta Northeast Floating Island, Number 16-B

--We found Elyos and Asmodian Daevas out here. They were too busy fighting each other to notice us. We captured them successfully.

Month 3 Day 36

Location: Reshanta Northeast Floating Island, Number 63-5

--As food runs out, Mantutu becomes harder and harder to control. His tamer is struggling more and more.

--Bhagwaninerk fell from the cannon today. His back was hurt pretty badly.

--We boarded a Black Cloud Traders ship passing by. Managed to capture cube craftsman Sidrunerk, which should prove useful.

Month 3 Day 43

Location: Reshanta East; Coordinates 32, 55

--The boiler malfunctioned, which has slowed us down a lot.

--Engineer Lahulahu and the captain fought severely. The chief engineer was so angry he brought Drana to the upper deck--the others ran below to keep away from the trouble.

--Chief Mate Menekiki and another sailor argued over which route we should take.

Month and Day Uncertain

Location: Reshanta East (approximately)

--As the time and space distorted, the navigator went out of order.

--Swirled by the distortion, the ship was pushed into the Aetheric Field of Aion. A strong shock hit the ship and some of the steam boilers under repair were damaged again. Some apprentice engineers were also injured.

--The captain declared a state of emergency.

Month and Day Uncertain

Location: Unknown Floating Island; Center of the Aetheric Field

--We hid the ship in the biggest floating island we could find within the Aetheric Field.

--A ship of Humans, either Elyos or Asmodians, passed by but did not notice us.

--Some sailors complained that the ship took an incorrect route, but Menekiki calmed them down. His sword helped with it.

Month and Day Uncertain

Location: Unknown Floating Island; Center of the Aetheric Field

--Elyos and Asmodian prisoners were getting noisy. After two missed meals, they settled down again.

--The captain said the repairs should have been done long ago, and left so angry that he shut himself up in the steering house.

--Bhagwaninerk fell off the cannon again. He managed to hurt his back in the same place as last time.

Month and Day Uncertain

Location: Unknown Floating Island; Center of the Aetheric Field

--A scout ship of the Black Cloud Traders caught the ship. We attacked, but failed to capture it.

--All sailors are on a full alert.

--The boilers are about ten percent repaired now.
