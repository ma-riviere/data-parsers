Aishi -dead

Bounty: 100,000 Kinah -collected

Charge: Atera pilgrims who visited the Miraju's Holy Ground.

Features: A mutant Wriggot. Very cautious and moves residence frequently. Likes dark and humid places.

How Caught: Spread ashes around the suspicious Aishi residence to identify the location. Killed by rigging an arrow trap outside its residence. Arrows coated with Yormunad Fluid.

Kratia -dead

Bounty: 800,000 Kinah -collected

Charge: Poisoned half of a village.

Features: It transforms to a Crynac when angry.

How Caught: Placed nets around the hideout. Burned dried Moonflower root to suffocate it.

Supervisor Kargas -dead

Bounty: 300,000 Kinah -collected

Charge: Poisoned the Mystic Spring of Anathe, and attacked passersby.

Features: Lepharist Revolutionary. Expert poisoner. Suspected of contaminating the Mystic Spring of Agairon.

How Caught: Disguised as a Wind Breeze merchant, and soaked herbs in Kargas's favored marketplace with Wriggot's bile. Waited for Kargas to create and consume his own potion. Death was long and torturous.

Furol the Gray -dead

Bounty: 3,000,000 Kinah -collected

Charge: Loot and Robbery

Features: Big and strong. More intelligent than most.

How to Catch: Dug several pits around his area, placed bamboo spears inside, and lured him out.
