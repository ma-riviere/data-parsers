- Altgard War Report -

From: Altgard Fortress Commander Suthran

To: Pandaemonium Governor Vidar

Here is my official report on our continuing campaigns in the Altgard region.

- Rebuilding Impetusium, which will serve as an outpost to defeat the Mau (ongoing)

- Subdued the MuMu Tribe and prevented them from refining Odella

- Allied with the Red Mane Tribe of Mau

- Sending Archons out for information gathering and prepare for attack (ongoing)

Anti-Sapien Combat Results

- Expanded Asmodian territory by conquering the MuMu Farmland

- Weakened the forces of the Black Claw Tribe by defeating their Elite Shamans and Seekers

- Killed Chieftain Kumbaron's minions

The Daeva who will deliver this report has played an important role in many of these events and can be considered worthy of your attention.

I trust you will be pleased with our achievements.

For the glory of Pandaemonium!
