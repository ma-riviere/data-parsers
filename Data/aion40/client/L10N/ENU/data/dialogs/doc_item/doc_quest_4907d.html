Subject Name: RM-108c

Procedural Log: Transplanted Slime's poison organ to 10 Humans. 5 subjects expired. Remaining subjects suffered high fever and blue skin discoloration.

Discoloration and fever subsequently diminished, being absent from all subjects by day 15. Four subjects suffered deafness, and dramatic reduction in intelligence. The sole exception will hereinafter be designated RM-108c, the others collectively RM-113b.

RM-108c and RM-113b were moved to the Bio Experiment Lab for detailed observation.

Subject Name: RM-78c

Procedural Log: Experiment goal is to enable subject to kill large numbers of victims at close range. Primary subject is a Daeva Warrior transferred from the Eltnen Bastion.

400 varieties of poison were injected or force-fed to the subject. The visual and auditory nerves were replaced with those from RM-5c and RM-60c. Transplanted the muscular tissues of Balaur collected from Beluslan to subject.

The subject was then moved to Ormenon's Room for cloning.

Subject Name: RM-138c

Procedural Log: New procedure designed to create good scouts and close quarters combatants. The cells from RM-78c were transplanted into the selected subjects. 30% of them died from side effects.

Testing determined that their vision and auditory senses are similar to RM-78c, but they only have combat skills similar to elite soldiers.

One Centurion was completed and sent to Azoturan Fortress for battle experience testing.
