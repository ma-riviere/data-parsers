Mystic Spring Management: Day 1

The Mystic Spring is filling up little by little.

It's a slow but consistent trickle, and we predict that the Mystic Spring will be filled in approximately ten days.

The water is clear and pure.

Mystic Spring Management: Day 15

The water filled up completely, but then went down a little. We are investigating the reason.

Although clear, the spring water smells like sand. Something else we must look into, I suppose.

Mystic Spring Management: Day 25

The water level of the Mystic Spring has returned to full.

Color and smell are both normal.

A peddler called Hippolytus stopped by, saying he was heading to the Eltnen Observatory on business and wanted to take a rest near the Mystic Spring.

Mystic Spring Management: Day 30

Water Level: Maximum

Water Condition: No abnormality in color or smell.

Note: Hippolytus is still camping near the Mystic Spring. He claimed that he was on a pilgrimage to the Kyola Temple. However, he looked around the temple the first day he arrived, and has been fixating on the Mystic Spring ever since. Seems shady. Perhaps I should let the fortress know.
