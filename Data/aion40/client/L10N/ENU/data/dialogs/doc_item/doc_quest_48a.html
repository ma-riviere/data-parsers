Dear Father,

Your letter has me very concerned.

But I have good news.

Do you remember the Daeva who acted as your messenger before?

That Daeva's great talents were immediately obvious to me.

I also sensed that the letter contained bad news.

Something so bad that I might need someone strong, who could help me.

So I read your letter quickly in front of the Daeva.

Its message was more horrible than I had thought.

So I asked the Daeva to do me a favor.

I knew they had a kind and generous spirit, because of the good deed they had already done for us.

I was proven right.

The Daeva defeated all those scary guys who had invaded the farm and forced you to steal the sacred Crystal.

I knew that it was a rare chance to meet a Daeva who has both the strength and the willingness to help us.

So I asked for help once more.

I had them kill the Lepharist boss, and retrieve the deed to our land, so that they cannot harass our family any more.

The Daeva gladly accepted my request.

Now no one will take our family estate or threaten you any longer.

All those bad guys and their bosses have been brought to justice by a kind and generous Daeva.

I would like to ask you to come over here and live with me, but I know that you will say no.

So I'll leave that alone, but I will ask you to do me a favor.

Daevas are neither authoritarian nor scary as you think.

There are many generous Daevas like the one who helped us, who worry about the welfare of us Humans.

All the villagers know that you are not greedy, and would never dare steal a sacred item, don't they?

Please go to see the highest Daeva of the village and tell him what happened to you.

When I was young, you taught me that a guilty conscience sickens one's mind.

I hope you will be absolved of all your sins and find peace of mind.

I will write you again soon.

Your son Qydro
