Healthy Noodles - Triora Pasta with Mookie and Cellatu

Ingredients: Mookie stems, Cellatu skins, Anevras, Triora noodles, bitter spices, splash of sticky liquid

Directions: 1. Remove thorns from plump Mookie stems, then julienne. 2. Place Cellatu skins in boiling water. Let cool until they reach a jelly-like consistency, then slice. 3. Tear Anevras into strips. 4. Cook Triora Noodles. 5. Mix sticky liquid and spices till seasoned. Add all other ingredients.
