Welcome to the Archon Studio, worthy Daeva!

The Archon Studios were built by Pandaemonium as a symbol of the respect that Asmodae affords its Daevas, and the loyalty received in return.

This manual will help you become accustomed to life in the Archon Studios.

- Coming Home -

When you received your Studio, you also were handed its key in the form of the Homeward Bound Skill Manual.

By learning this skill, you ensure that home is never far away. Homeward Bound can return you to your Studio from any point in Atreia.

Your home can also be accessed through the Studio front gates.

- Building Maintenance Costs -

The Maintenance Costs of the Studios are paid by Pandaemonium.

However, if you upgrade your housing, you will be responsible for the Building Maintenance Costs.

- Moving Out -

If you choose to purchase a different home, your Studio will be reclaimed.

However, if circumstances ever leave you without a house, speak to Sarrik and you can receive Studio housing again.

- Making Yourself at Home -

Fill your home with decorative items and souvenirs from your travels.

When in your home, click on the house icon near your skills to arrange items.

- Editing Scripts -

You can edit the scripts for the butler or installations to apply more functions freely.

In the Butler Menu in your house, click Edit Script to start editing.

- Leaving Home -

The gates on either side of your porch allow you to exit your Studio.
