Dear Polinia,

My dearest cousin, I know you are continuing to make me proud.

I hope everything is well.

It is such a long time since I wrote anything that I feel awkward holding a pen.

I am sending this to you with a youngster who brought me an old book.

This stranger had many questions.

They helped me out a great deal, so there must be something important about this book.

However, old books are not at all in my line as you know.

That's why I'm recommending them to an expert. Don't be modest; I know you too well.

This youngster is very polite and sincere.

I know you are busy, but I'm sure it won't take too much of your time.

I hope you and all priests are getting along well.

-Kales
