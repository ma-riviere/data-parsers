Day 17

The lost city of Heironopolis, at last! Who knows what secrets lurk in these abandoned ruins?

So far just dust and pebbles, actually. In truth I feel rather uneasy here, like the ghosts of the Superans still live in their crumbled homes and watch me through the windows.

Day 19

The Poya Jungle is amazing! So green, so full of life!

I've been trying to make sketches of some of the more fascinating creatures but I'm afraid my drawing skills are rather limited. I shall have to recommend the place to the Sanctum Naturalist's Society.

Although if there's one animal I don't much like, its the Klaws.

Day 25

I'm in someplace completely different. Weird, right?
