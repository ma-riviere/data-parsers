The Pastoral Promise of Poeta

Poeta has never been accused of resembling a bustling metropolis, but is a must-visit destination for the more provincially inclined.

A Rustic Flavor

In the admittedly little-contested field of Poeta's cuisine, chef Uno emerges as a clear winner. For those who find the delicacies of Sanctum too rich for their palate, Uno's artisanal Poeta Bread has a charmingly rustic presentation and a hearty flavor that conjures up a certain rural nostalgia.

The Good Life

Where better to spend hours taking in Poeta's beautiful sunsets than from Feira's Dock? Once you've spent an afternoon casting your line into the still waters of Cliona Lake, guard duty at New Heiron Gate won't seem so tedious. And, if you're extremely lucky, perhaps the master fisher herself, Feira, will give you some gracious pointers on your technique!

Don't forget to pick up a souvenir. Memories fade, but garishly printed postcards never will!
