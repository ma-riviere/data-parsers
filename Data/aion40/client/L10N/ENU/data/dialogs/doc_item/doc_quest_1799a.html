100-slot Cube Crafting Method

Materials:

6 square Orichalcum plates, 10 rectangular Adamantium plates with 7 grooves, 8 rectangular Adamantium plates with 9 grooves, and 2 hidden hinges.

Tools: Gold plate, Aether iron, Vindachinerk press, Aether injector

Note: Orichalcum and Adamantium plates must comply with Vindachinerk standards.

Crafting Method

1. Assemble Adamantium plates in a lattice. Press them to maximum tolerance.

2. Connect 5 Orichalcum plates with gold plate and put them together like an open box. Place the lattice made in Step 1 inside the box diagonally and nail the hinges on the door.

3. Inject Aether into the box using Aether injector, press the box with 100 Vindachinerk of force, and inject Aether once more.
