Book of Record

(Opening this book, you hear testimony by Savinus followed by Gelon--both Reian tribesmen.)

I am Savinus, a Reian tribesman. It was many, many years ago, before the great fall of the night curtain.

We were Atreians just like you, and we worshipped Siel as you do. Siel loved us very much and bestowed upon us the name "The People of Siel's Spear."

The Lady of Time told us to infiltrate Balaurea and clear the land of its savages, but the Cataclysm happened during the war...tore the world into two halves.

It was many generations ago. Those Reians that survived the Cataclysm were stuck in Balaurea. Our fathers settled in Inggison.

We have endured this land for a long, long time. And I believe this is how the misunderstanding came about. We are not one with the Balaur--we simply share the same land.

I hope your people will come to know this about us.

Ignorant Elyos! To think a simple book will set the record straight! Can its pages undo the deaths of thousands of my people? I did not think so.

Is this...is this recording now? Oh...all right...here we go. I am Gelon. My people fought for Siel and no one else. She was inside the Aetheric Field when we entered Balaurea.

And during the great divide, many of my people perished. But we fought for Atreia...for the protection of Atreia. But no one remembers....

You must remember, Humans were here long before Elysea and Asmodae were but a glimmer of an idea. And we served alongside them in peace.

We, the Reian tribe, are the only existing race who remembers the days of peace.
