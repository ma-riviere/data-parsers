Who's Who: Benes

A Daeva of Divine Clothing who operates a Fine Boutique on Divine Road in Sanctum. Benes began studying needlework as Andu's apprentice at the age of seven. Naturally gifted, she rose quickly through the Handicrafting ranks and opened her own shop just a short 15 years later.

With her unrivaled color-coordination abilities, it's no wonder that Bennes is called "the magician of colors." Her brilliantly colored Stenon dress remains a top seller to this day, despite the twenty-year-old design. A true classic.

Benes is the author of the bestsellers "Now This is Needlework", "Living as a Single Woman", and "A Fashion for Any Occasion".
