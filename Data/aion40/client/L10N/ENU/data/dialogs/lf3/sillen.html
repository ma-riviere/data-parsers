Welcome, Daeva.

Some people call me "The Luckiest Woman".

It's not really my fault. Once I was captured by Kerubs and a wandering Daeva saved me. Then I fell from a cliff and survived because my clothes got caught in the branch of a tree.

So when people ask me how I got stuck in such an awful place, I say "Just lucky I guess!"

 "What's your secret?" "Good luck."

The secret is...well, I don't know.

Maybe it's because I don't have a fear of death? I know it's strange for a Human, but the thought of dying doesn't bother me. If I go to join the flow of Aether, it's Aion's will, and not something to be feared.

Or maybe...I'm just really, really lucky.

 "You never know."
