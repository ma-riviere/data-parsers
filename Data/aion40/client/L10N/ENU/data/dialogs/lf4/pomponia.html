Don't think of Soteria Sanctuary as a town hastily built on top of an old, shabby reliquary. See it for what it really is: perfectly situated for holding off Balaur and Asmodians.

Ragged mountains shield us from Drakan eyes. A nearby fissure and a desolate desert protect us from stealth attacks. The ground we stand on even survived Tiamat's butchery intact.

Like the Reian Tribe's ancestral protectors, we use the dilapidated village to mask our attack force!
