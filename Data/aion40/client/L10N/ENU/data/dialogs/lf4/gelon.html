When I was a boy...

When I was a boy, before the Cataclysm, even then the Elyos had a noble spirit. And this temple, in its shining glory, was so loved that it sparkled brighter than Aion's Eye, and its visitors piled on top of one another until newcomers' heads touched the ceiling.

Well, it doesn't look the same now, of course.

 "I guess it's pretty enough."
