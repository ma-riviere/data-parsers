Asmo was a big, strong, sharp-clawed Asmodian Daeva. One fine day in the Abyss, she met a skinny, sunburned, fancily-dressed Elyos Daeva.

"My name is Ellie," said the Elyos, "and I don't think we should fight each other. I think we should be friends!"

"Okay," said Asmo, smiling. "I promise I won't try to kill you."

"Since we're friends now," Asmo said, "let me give you a few makeup tips."

"Wow! I'd love some!" said Ellie. "I'm always looking for ways to make myself prettier!"

Asmo handed Ellie a vial of poison powder. "Take this. It makes great lipstick."

Ellie gladly took the poison powder and smeared it on her lips. She took out a mirror and admired herself.

"You're right! I look great!" she said. And then she dropped dead.

Asmo flew to the Obelisk to wait for the stupid Elyos to come back to life. She wanted to have more fun with her.

When Ellie came back to life, she waved to Asmo. "It's so good to see you again!" she said. "You're my new best friend!"

"Of course I am," said Asmo. "But do you see that Balaur on that island over there? I heard him say he wants to be your friend too."

"Really? That's wonderful! I always wanted a Balaur friend!" And Ellie flew off to say hello to the Balaur.

She flew down and landed right next to the Balaur. He looked at her in surprise.

"Hello, Mr. Balaur!" she said. "Will you be my friend?" In answer, the Balaur drove his axe into Ellie's skull. Her brains splattered all over the island.

Asmo waited at the Obelisk for Ellie to come back to life. She wanted to have even more fun with her.

"That Balaur wasn't a very good friend," Ellie said when she came back to life.

"Maybe he didn't think you looked pretty enough," said Asmo. "After all, your makeup's gone now."

"But you don't need makeup to look pretty. You just need the right light. Like over there, right next to that sun."

"That's great advice! Thanks!" said Ellie, as she took wing and started flying towards the burning red sun.

Asmo watched as the stupid Elyos flew off to her death. She smiled as she watched Ellie burst into flames.

She laughed and laughed at the thought of her beautiful skin being charred black. She waited by the Obelisk for Ellie to come back to life.

But Ellie had flown too far out from the Obelisk. Her soul would be banished forever.

Asmo could have no more fun with her.
