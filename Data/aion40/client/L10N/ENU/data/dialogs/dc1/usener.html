Azphelumbra!

How did I become a master craftsman? Well, my family were merchants, but I was never interested in business. I always liked to make things with my hands.

Weapons especially fascinated me, as a practical art. One must make a sword functional, keen, well-balanced, strong, but also beautiful.

I was also proud to do something to help us win the war. I've since come to specialize in restoring magic swords like...Agrif's Rage.

 "Agrif's Rage?" "Thanks, I must go."

It's is a very powerful magic weapon created by my master Agrif for the victory of Asmodae, after his family was slaughtered by Elyos.

But his grief and anger were so powerful that it bound to the sword and inhabits anyone who wields it.

That's why an Empyrean Lord sundered the weapon and sealed its power away.

 "I wonder where it is..."
