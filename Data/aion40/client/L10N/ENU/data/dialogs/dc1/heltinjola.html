Ah, the beauty of Pandaemonium!

Why are some chosen to ascend while others must die? My parents, my brothers and sisters--they all ascended.

But I remain mortal. My family is greatly saddened that I'll grow old and die while they stay young forever.

But I remind them that Asmodians do not waste time weeping over what we cannot change. If I am not to be a Daeva, my destiny lies elsewhere.

 "Serve Asmodae well."
