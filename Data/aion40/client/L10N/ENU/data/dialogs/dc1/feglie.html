Hail!

Strategically speaking, the Airship Dock is just about the most important place in Pandaemonium.

Almost every person and crate comes through here on its way into or out of the city.

That makes this a prestigious post, but there's a lot of pressure too. Some of the other guards are a bit overenthusiastic about their duties. Security is important, but there's no need to hassle every single person, you know?

 "Restraint is a virtue."
