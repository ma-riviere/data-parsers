Pandaemonium stirs up the strangest feelings. I visit here often purchasing supplies, but I never quite get used to it.

It's a unique mixture--the most spectacular sights and sounds to be found anywhere in Atreia, yet...something is missing. For all its beauty, I don't think Pandaemonium could ever feel like home.
