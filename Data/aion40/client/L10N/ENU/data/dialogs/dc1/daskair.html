 "Who did you love?" "Doesn't it always?"

I was determined not to make the same mistake again, but...I just can't help myself.

 "So who's this new love?" "That's too bad."

She's from the Vanahal District, and she won't let anyone forget it. Mention her name to anyone in Pandaemonium, and they'll tell you the same thing: keep away.

But what can I do? It's just like before. I can't stop thinking about her. Even after all I've been through, I would gladly give her every last Kinah I have.
