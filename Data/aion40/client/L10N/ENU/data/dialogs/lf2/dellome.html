May the light guide your path!

Agairon Village is in a terrible state. In the old days, this village teemed with food and drink and fruit--oh Daeva! The fruit!

You can't imagine how prosperous it used to be! Yet even then, desert was encroaching and we prospered only because of our sweat and toil. These young people today just don't want to work--that's why we're failing, I just know it.

 "May you live long."
