Do Asmodae proud!

I've been sent to many postings in my career. Everywhere I've gone, there has been misery and trouble.

But here.... This is one of the worst. Between Balaur rampages, Drana contamination, the Sapiens and Reians oppressed....

Still, I don't give in to despair. If I did, I could never contribute to solving these problems.

 "A wise attitude."
