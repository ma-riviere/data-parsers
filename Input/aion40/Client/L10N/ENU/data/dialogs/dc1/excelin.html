May the night-winds sing your glory, Daeva!

All my life, I've devoted myself to learning. Even before my ascension, I loved to bury myself in books. But now I wonder if I've been wasting my time all along.

I've studied so much, and yet none of it applies to my real life. None of it matters!

All I've ever done is study...as if that was the same as really living. I thought I knew all about the world, when all I really know is useless words! What monstrous arrogance!

 "What happened?" "Tell me later, okay?"

A woman asked me how a mechanical loom works. So, I recited all the things I've read about looms in my books. I explained about the shuttle, thread tension, how the power of Aether is worked in....

And her response struck me like lightning. She said, "I had no idea you knew so much about weaving, Excelin! I wish you'd make me a dress!"

She meant well, but it was like a slap in the face. I can tell you the names of Pandaemonium's most famous artists, explain how pigment is made, but I've never even picked up a brush! I'm a colossal fraud!
