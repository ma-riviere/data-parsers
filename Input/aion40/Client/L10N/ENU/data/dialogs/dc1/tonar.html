Blood for blood, young Daeva...

I had prepared to die. My mind was made up, I simply thought--I was old, I was going to die soon, but my family would be there to see me go.

When I ascended they were all so happy that I wouldn't die. I had to watch all them die instead--and I'm stuck in this old body forever.

I still don't know why Aion chose me to ascend. I just don't know what good an old man can do.

 "Aion's will is mysterious."
