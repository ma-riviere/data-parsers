The weather here in Pernon is unpredictable.

Sun, then sudden rain. Clear, then sudden mist. Calm, then sudden wind. And then, just as suddenly, calm again.

It's almost certainly because of the varied landscape. Mountains, lowlands, inlets and sea--they affect wind currents and temperatures quite drastically.

But despite these drastic changes, Pernon is most often temperate and mild. It's very interesting, really.
