For what reason have I been awoken?

The power to counter Siel's Relics is greater than even a Daevic mind can bear.

It begs to be used, keens from within the chambers of your heart to be unleashed. But it does not discriminate between good and ill. You must not bow to it, let it direct you.

It... it nearly destroyed me. I created this space, my own sacred realm, so that I might focus all my energy on preserving it instead of spending it on minor grudges and daily frustrations.

Even now, after my endless slumber, I am tired. So, so tired.

 "Rest, Oriata, and I will carry your burden awhile."
