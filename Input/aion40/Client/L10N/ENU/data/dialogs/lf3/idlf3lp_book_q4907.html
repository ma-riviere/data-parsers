Subject Name: RM-108c

Procedural Log: Transplanted Slime's poison organ to 10 Humans. 5 subjects expired. Remaining subjects suffered high fever and blue skin discoloration.

Discoloration and fever subsequently diminished, being absent from all subjects by day 15. Four subjects suffered deafness, and dramatic reduction in intelligence. The sole exception will hereinafter be designated RM-108c, the others collectively RM-113b.

RM-108c and RM-113b were moved to the Bio Experiment Lab for detailed observation.

Procedural Log: Experiment goal is to enable subject to kill large numbers of the enemy at close range. Primary subject is a Daeva Warrior transferred from the Eltnen Bastion.

400 varieties of poison were injected or force-fed to the subject. Subject's visual and auditory nerves were replaced with tissue from RM-5c and RM-60c. Balaur muscle tissue successfully transplanted to subject.

On recovery, the subject was moved to Ormenon's Room to begin cloning.

Subject Name: RM-138c

Procedural Log: New procedure designed to create ideal close-quarters fighters. Selected tissues from RM-78c were transplanted into selected subjects. 30% died from various side effects.

Testing determined that subjects' vision and auditory senses are similar to RM-78c, but combat skills are only equal to elite soldiers.

One Centurion was completed and sent to Azoturan Fortress for battle experience testing.
