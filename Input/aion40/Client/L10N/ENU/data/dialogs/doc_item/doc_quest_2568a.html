My dear Freyja,

How's life in Beluslan? It probably isn't easy keeping warm over there. You may be wondering why your brother is suddenly sending you this letter. Well, I'd like to ask a special favor.

Actually I am making a special mace, and I think that mineral may help me. I'm counting on you.

From Pandaemonium, Your brother Freyr.
