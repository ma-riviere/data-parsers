If ye Weapons be as stronge as I do Hope, then None shalle unlocke my Strongholde.

Theyre Strengthe shalle be Unmatchedde.

Ye Firste Weapon shall be ye Budde of Lyfe.

Ye Second Weapon shall be ye Flame of Inspyration.

Ye Thyrde Weapon shall be ye Flowre of Hope.

Ye Fourth Weapon shall be ye Maske of Ultymate Powre.

All four Orbs shall be kepte Safe, the Keyes to my Laborytorie helde by Guardyns of Stone.
