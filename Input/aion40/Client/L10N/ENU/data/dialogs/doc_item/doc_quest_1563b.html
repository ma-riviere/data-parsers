100-slot Cube Crafting Method

Materials: 6 square Orichalcum plates, 10 rectangular Adamantium plates with 7 grooves, 8 rectangular Adamantium plates with 9 grooves, and 2 hidden hinges.

Tools: Gold plate, Aether iron, Vindachinerk press, Aether injector.

Note: The sizes of Orichalcum and Adamantium plates must comply with Vindachinerk standards.

Crafting Method

1. Assemble Adamantium plates in a lattice form. Place them under the press and process them both horizontally and vertically.

2. Connect 5 Orichalcum plates with the gold plate and put them together like an open box. Place the lattice made in Step 1 inside the box diagonally and nail the hinges on the door.

3. Inject Aether into the box using the Aether injector, press six sides of the box with the same force and inject Aether once more.
