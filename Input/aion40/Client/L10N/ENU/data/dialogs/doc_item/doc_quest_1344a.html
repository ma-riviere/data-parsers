Eracus Desert Scout Report

1. Morphinos Massacre

The Morphinos in the Eracus Desert have all been killed. Investigations have revealed that they were attacked by Rattletail Scolopens.

2. Dartas Village

The Dartas Village has been swallowed entirely by the desert, and is now nearly buried.

All residents have evacuated and we are escorting the refugees to safety.

3. Gryphu?

Large numbers of Gryphu have been sighted in some parts of the desert. We are investigating the reason.

4. Ksellid Attacks

Ksellids, which have never been known to threaten Humans, have become hostile.

Investigation suggests they may have accidentally harmed Humans while trying to capture Speedy.

We eliminated Speedy, and ordered the locals to keep their distance from the Ksellids.

5. Mystic Spring Status

Most of the Mystic Springs are drying up, though the Daevas of Life do their best to take care of them.

It may be difficult to stop the desertification of the Eltnen region unless the Mystic Springs return to normal.
