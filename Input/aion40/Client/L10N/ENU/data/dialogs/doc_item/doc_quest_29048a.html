Emotions cannot be hidden, disguised, or simulated.

Those who fake emotion may fool some, but not all. After all, emotion is from the soul, and eyes are the window to that soul.

So always be true to yourself and others. Lying is a fool's escape that creates complications which must be rectified later. Better to be honest in all things: in your speech, mannerisms, and movement, whether you are standing, running, jumping, or resting.

These first lessons can be read, but the rest must be taught.

- Seriphim, Preceptor of Emotion
