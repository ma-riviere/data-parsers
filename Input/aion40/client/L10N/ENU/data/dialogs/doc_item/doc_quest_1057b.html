Entry 93 -- The Research Center Chairman said my research results were impressive, and that he would promote me to the Secret Laboratory.

They finally approved my work! If things keep going this well, I might be able to advance my position in the Revolution more quickly than I anticipated.

Still , I can't stop wondering what are they studying in the Secret Laboratory? I must know!

Entry 121 -- The Secret Laboratory was hidden behind the Rotron Experiment Lab in the northern part of the Research Center. Gatekeeper Kirrin was standing in front of the door looking fearsome.

It didn't take me long to realize that they won't let me in on everything, even though I work in the Secret Laboratory.

It seems that there are many layers of secrecy here.

Entry 140 -- They've given me access to the Bio Experiment Lab. It's behind a gate guarded by Karnifs.

So far, they've performed experiments on Pretors and Mudthorns. They gave me a crystal with mysterious properties and ordered me to observe what effect it has on the Rotrons.

My work here might take me some time. I'm not sure what the desired result is.

Entry 146 -- According to rumor, there's only supposed to be one lab beyond this one.

It is called Ormenon's Room, and two doors lead off it. Few people have access to it.

What kind of research warrants such high security? I worry that my curiosity may lead to trouble, but it's in my nature. It's the whole reason I became a researcher in the first place--to find things out!

Entry 159 -- I made friends with a researcher named Nargis who used to work in the Mudthorn Laboratory and was recently posted here.

He hasn't had much difficulty adjusting, but I think he's as curious as I am about the secret research in Ormenon's Room.

At least now I have someone I can talk to about it. Quietly.

Entry 165 -- Something is going on with the senior researchers. I don't know exactly what the problem is, but they look concerned. They gather in twos and threes, and abruptly stop talking when we get close.

I've heard more than one of them say "RM-78c," though.

It sounds like one of the code-names we give to synthesized substances. I asked Nargis about it, and he's never heard of it before. I wonder what has them all so worried.

Entry 168 -- Looking back over this journal it seems obvious that I should have been more worried. I feel like I've been misled.

They said that Lord Lephar would become an Empyrean Lord, and put an end to all the chaos in Atreia. Only, I recently got some time off and managed to get outside for the first time in ages, and the world seemed more chaotic than ever.

The Rotrons are still terrorizing people. I believed that my research would help others, but I'm starting to think I was wrong.

Entry 173 -- I contrived to overhear a conversation between the Research Center Chairman and the senior researchers. I told Nargis what they said, and he was as shocked as I.

I have to get away from the Lepharists as quickly as possible. RM-78c isn't a synthesized chemical at all--it's a monster!

The Revolution I dreamed of is not what the Lepharists stand for. Everything I've done for the Revolution for the last ten years...it's all been in vain.
