Necessary Materials for Weapon Enhancement

In order to achieve the correct environment for tempering, use Jotun's Flux with a special Refining Agent.

Note--Refining Agents derived from Arachnas are best. Be aware that these will be poisonous.

Note 2--The Flux is optional, but doubles the effective power of the Refining Agent.

Tempering Sequence

1. Set the furnace to 2nd degree high.

2. Use one Jotun Flux and half of the special Refining Agent.

3. Wait for the Flux to melt and produce steam.

4. Add the remainder of the special Refining Agent.

5. Heat weapon until red-hot.

6. Once heated thoroughly, beat the weapon, temper, and repeat.
