- First Chapter -

An ominous blood-red shadow will come to blanket Atreia in darkness.

The descendants of the cursed Tower will tremble in fear. Their wings are weak against the blood-red darkness of eternity. Even the warring twins siding under one-winged cohesion will struggle against the wrath of the Dragon Lords.

May eternal glory and power be with the great Dragon Lords, the Kings of Blood and Wrath!

- Second Chapter -

Those weak, fragile wings slowly stir up the winds in a sea of blood-red darkness. The winds from the cadenced flapping turn into a fury of tornadoes to whip away the darkness.

In the midst of that darkness, Mighty Wings appear. Carried high by the roar of the people, they soar towards the Infinity Throne.

- Third Chapter -

As is destined, the Flames of Retribution intercept those Wings. But the Wings - strengthened by the people - know no retreat, pressing forward.

The Wings ablaze in flames, unleashes a fury of devastation on the stronghold, reducing it to ashes.

Woe is me...! Even the blood-red throne falls short of destiny...

- Fourth Chapter -

The Wings burn the Crown, as the Master of the Crown dies an excruciatingly slow and painful death.

Alas! Even that which is eternal in power and glory, turns to ashes!

The Four Crowns can only lament at the sight of the befallen Crown.
