Congratulations on your new Studio!

This cozy Studio is provided by Sanctum as a reward to all its loyal Daevas, no matter their financial situation.

Please read this Manual carefully so you may enjoy your life in Oriel to its fullest.

- Entering Your Home -

When you get a Studio, you'll receive a tome that teaches the Homeward Bound skill.

This skill creates a portal through which you can enter your home no matter where you are.

You can also use the Studio Entrance in Arcada State to return to your Studio.

- Building Maintenance Costs -

Studios are completely free to live in, thanks to the generosity of Sanctum.

However, if you decide to purchase your own detatched house, you will need to pay maintenance costs regularly.

- Moving Out of a House -

Purchasing your own home will, of course, cause you to lose ownership of your free Studio.

However, if you should then lose ownership of your detatched home, your Studio will become available once more. Speak to Parrine to reclaim it.

- Decorating -

Decorative items such as furniture or outdoor installations help make a house into a home, reflective of your tastes.

While inside your home, click the house icon displayed at the bottom right to start decorating.

- Editing Scripts -

You can edit the scripts for your butler or decorations to apply more functions freely.

In the Butler Menu in your house, click Edit Script to start editing.

- Exiting Your Studio -

The balcony outside your Studio has two gates, one at each end.

Take either to leave your Studio and appear at the Studio Entrance in Arcada State.
