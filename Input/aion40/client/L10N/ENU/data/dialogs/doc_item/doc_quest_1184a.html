The Water Spirits and Wind Spirits near Miraju's Holy Ground have become violent for unknown reasons.

They attack pilgrims to the Ardus Shrine. We request that all pilgrims be informed of the threat and advised to stay away.

There seems to be nothing abnormal in the flow of Aether, and this far from the contaminated forest, it's unlikely that toxins are causing it.

We're so short-handed here it seems unlikely our investigations will turn anything up--all our men are busy fighting the spirits.

If the situation doesn't improve, it may be best to blockade Miraju's Holy Ground and prohibit access.
