I've heard stories of battle, but it all seems so far away when you walk in beautiful Sanctum.

I've walked these streets many times since my ascension. I'm at peace now more than I ever was as a Human.

Of course I understand it's temporary. Soon I'll receive my first mission, and I'll have to leave Sanctum, possibly forever.

Even if I return, I'm not sure I'll ever be at peace like this again. I've seen what battle does to us. But if that's my duty, then so be it. A loyal Daeva must sacrifice for the good of all Elyos.

 "Faith and arms."
