What does an Information Officer do, you ask? Well, I'm glad to tell you!

We announce policies and news from Pandaemonium to the citizens. I take it quite seriously.

Of course we also deliver the opinions of the citizens to the priest or the fortress commander. It's important for those in charge to understand how the citizens feel.

Most Information Officers move on to different jobs, though, because of the low pay.

 "Your salary is low?" "I must be going now."

It is, but for me it's not a problem. I already own a Tripeed farm near Alsig village, which supports me fine.

I took this job to meet powerful people, and to understand the politics of government better. I may want to become a higher official someday....

 "Best of luck with that."
