May time be on your side.

I wasn't always a blacksmith, you know. Before the Cataclysm, I made timepieces and clockwork toys.

But when the Millenium War started, I volunteered to turn my skills towards making necessary arms for the struggle against the Balaur.

I don't regret it for a moment, but...it's been so long since I practiced my old craft. I still miss it.
